Our Specialized Services

We focus on building foundational motor skills and coordination essential for your child's success.

Fine Motor Skills

Developing dexterity and hand-eye coordination for tasks like writing and cutting.

Gross Motor Skills

Improving balance, strength, and coordination for walking, running, and physical play.

Sensory Integration

Helping children process and react appropriately to information received through the senses.

Self-Care Routines

Teaching independence in daily tasks such as dressing, bathing, and feeding themselves.

Cognitive Skills

Enhancing problem-solving, planning, memory, and attention during learning activities.

Social Interaction

Developing essential peer-to-peer communication and play skills in a structured setting.
