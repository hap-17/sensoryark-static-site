Our Specialized Services

We provide personalized, play-based therapy that nurtures each child’s growth in meaningful ways

Sensory Processing & Regulation

Supporting calmness, attention, and body awareness through sensory-based activities.

Fine Motor Skills

Developing hand strength, coordination, and precision for writing, dressing, feeding, and play.

Gross Motor & Core Strength

Building balance, posture, coordination, and overall physical confidence.

Self-Care & Independence

Guiding children through daily routines such as dressing, toileting, feeding, and grooming.

Executive & Cognitive Functioning Skills

Strengthening attention, planning, organization, memory, and problem-solving to support learning and daily activities.

Social Skills & Play

Encouraging connection, teamwork, and peer interaction through structured and free play.

School Readiness & Learning Skills

Preparing children with focus, participation strategies, and pre-academic foundations.

Emotional Regulation & Confidence Building

Helping children identify feelings, develop coping tools, and build self-esteem in a supportive environment.
